Buy an RSVP and you could see Oregon play in the 2026 College Football Playoff National Championship on Monday, January 19, 2026 at Hard Rock Stadium in Miami.

An RSVP is NOT A TICKET, but guarantees access to a ticket at CFP-RSVP ticket prices if your team advances to the 2026 College Football Playoff National Championship. There are no refunds for your RSVP if your team does not advance. If your team advances, you will not be charged for your ticket until you make the purchase.

Price differences are due to fans reselling their RSVPs, which can be listed at the price of their choice.
